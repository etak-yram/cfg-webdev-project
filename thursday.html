Founded by John “Long John” MacDonald in 1825, Ben Nevis Distillery is one of the oldest licensed distilleries in Scotland. Nestled at the foot of Britain's highest mountain, Ben Nevis, the distillery has been crafting malt whisky from the highest water source in Scotland for nearly two hundred years.


We will take part in a tour of the distillery, followed by a tasting session!
